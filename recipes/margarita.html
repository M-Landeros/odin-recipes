Margarita

Description

A popular cocktail all over the world, the margarita or marg is a classic refreshing cocktail. Thought to have started as a variation of the Daisy drink, the margarita swapped the brandy for tequila and lime juice over lemon juice.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup of kosher salt or coarse sea salt
  • 1 or 2 lime wedges
  • 4 oz blanco tequila
  • 2 oz triple sec
  • 1 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
  • Ice

Directions:

  1. Place salt on a shallow plate. Rub ice or glass rim with lime wedge, then dip into salt to coat.
  2. Divide tequila, triple sec, and lime juice between prepared glasses and stir to combine. Top with ice and garnish with a wedge of lime
