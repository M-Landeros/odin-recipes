Steak tacos

Description

A carne asada taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn tortilla topped with a grilled and sliced beef such as skirt or flank steak. The taco is usually garnished with various condiments such as cilantro, onions, hot sauce, and lime juice. Tacos are a popular form of Mexican street food.

Ingredients
  • 2 lb Skirt or flank steak
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1/4 cup light beer
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 7 Garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Corn tortillas (regular size or street taco size)
  • Cilantro
  • White onion
  • lime
  • Hot sauce (red or green)
Instructions:
  1. In a large bowl, combine the lime and orange juice, light beer,soy sauce, olive oil, cilantro, white onion, and garlic cloves to form a marinade.
  2. Season the steak with cumin, salt, and black pepper and place in the marinade. Set aside in fridge for 24 hrs.
  3. Dice a white onion, and finely chop some cilantro. Place in separate small bowls or combine well in a single bowl. Cut a few limes into fourths.
  4. After the steak has been well marinated, heat up cast iron skillet. Use a little bit of oil in the skillet such as canola, and place the steak on the hot skillet together with the onions and garlic cloves.
  5. After a few minutes, flip the steak over and cook the other side.
  6. Once the steak is fully cooked, remove from skillet and cut into very small cubes or chunks. Remember to cut against the grain!
  7. Now take some tortillas (1 or 2 per taco) and dip in the juices leftover in the skillet. Warm up the tortillas over an open flame, griddle, or in the same skillet as before.
  8. Once the tortillas are nice and hot, it is time to assemble our tacos.
  9. Take 1 or two tortillas and add steak, garnish with diced onions and cilantro. Top it off with your choice of hot sauce and a squeeze of lime juice.
  10. Enjoy!
